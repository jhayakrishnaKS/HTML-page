Remembering Pele

About pele

Pelé began playing for Santos at age 15 and the Brazil national team at 16. During his international career, he won three FIFA World Cups: 1958, 1962 and 1970, the only player to do so and the youngest player to win a World Cup

Football Career

In Brazil, he was hailed as a national hero for his accomplishments in football and for his outspoken support of policies that improve the social conditions of the poor

Legacy

Throughout his career and in his retirement, Pelé received numerous individual and team awards for his performance on the field, his record-breaking achievements,and his legacy in the sport.
